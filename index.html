Fun Facts about TRON

  • TRON was originally inspired by the Pong video game.
  • Some Disney animators refused to work on it. It was said they felt threatened by the potential for computers to take their jobs.
  • The CGI used in the movie took only 2 megabytes of memory.
  • The characters of TRON use flying discs because the creators had a no-guns policy out of fear of encouraging violence in children.
  • TRON inspired one animator who would go on to create an entire studio based on CG animation: John Lasseter.
